From Calcium to Care: Key Factors Influencing Eggshell Quality in Layers

By M. Naeem

The latest issue of NutriNews International brings a wealth of information on animal nutrition and health, featuring research on the effects of vitamins D, E, and C on pig health, updates on feedstuff evaluation for precision nutrition, and strategies for improving eggshell quality in poultry. Other highlights include nutrition approaches to extend layer lifecycles, the importance of methionine in poultry diets for antioxidant protection, and a closer look at grain processing in cattle feeding—plus many other fascinating topics. This issue offers valuable insights across multiple species and areas of animal nutrition—don’t miss it!
